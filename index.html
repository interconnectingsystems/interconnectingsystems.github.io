Interconnecting systems

The air we breathe, the water we drink, the food we eat, the clothes we wear, the place we live in our home and environment (all the plants, animals and living beings around us), the energy we use, our family and friends they are all interconnected. Small changes now might have considerable consequences in the future. We need to reconnect ourselves to the earth, to nature, to the systems that enable us to live.

We are interconnecting systems.
